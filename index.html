Hi, I'm Mercy, a Product Designer and a Frontend Developer

I didn't grow up knowing I'd end up here, crafting interfaces, solving problems, and obsessing over margins. But curiosity's always been my thing. I've always asked why? and what if?

I care about how things work, sure but even more about how they feel. Whether I'm designing flows or coding them into life, I'm always thinking about the humans on the other side of the screen(the users).

I studied Human Anatomy. I didn't come from a "design background." Weird switch? Maybe. But honestly, it makes sense. I've always been obsessed with how things work: bodies, products, systems, you name it. Curiosity won the day and I pivoted mid-degree I earned.

That same curiosity is what led me here; designing digital experiences that don't just look pretty, but actually work for people. I'm not just pushing pixels or making things "pop." I dig into real business problems, user pain points, and competitor gaps to build products that convert, retain, and delight. I've helped boost product satisfaction by 15%, thanks to user research, prototyping, and testing that's rooted in empathy and clarity.

Beyond product design and frontend development, I also create visual identities and logos that give brands a voice and feeling at first glance. Because great design isn't just seen, it's felt.

Over the years, I've worn many hats from virtual assistant to customer support rep to product manager and each role sharpened my empathy, systems thinking, and ability to truly collaborate.
